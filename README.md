- **Secondary**: Desert copper/terracotta (#c2813a) — red rock warmth
- **Display Font**: Fraunces (organic variable serif) | **Body Font**: DM Sans

- **Loyalty Currency**: Trail Points

## Key Brand Details
- Founded 2013 by Jonathan Buford and Patrick Ware
- Named world's best new brewery by RateBeer 2016
- Locations: Gilbert brewpub (721 N Arizona Ave), DTPHX beer garden (201 E Roosevelt St)
- Arizona terroir philosophy — Sinagua Malt (local), Hayden Flour Mills heritage grains, Agritopia Farm citrus/peaches, foraged ingredients, Schnepf Farms, Zak's Chocolate, Peixoto Coffee
- Wilderness lodge aesthetic — knotty pine, log-cut bars, tree growing through dining room, mist systems on patios
- AZ grass-fed beef burgers, duck fat fries, meatloaf sliders with beer wort gravy
- French oak foeders for wild/sour program
- Badges themed around hiking: Trailhead, Day Hiker, Backcountry, Summit, Wilderness Legend
